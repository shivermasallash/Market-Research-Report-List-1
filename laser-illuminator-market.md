<p><ul><li>Visible Light Laser Illumination</li><li>Infrared Laser Illumination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser illuminator market is divided into two primary types: visible light laser illumination and infrared laser illumination. Visible light lasers emit light wavelengths that can be seen by the human eye, making them suitable for applications like presentations and displays. In contrast, infrared laser illumination operates outside the visible spectrum, providing covert illumination for night vision and surveillance systems. Both types serve distinct purposes across various industries, including security, entertainment, and automotive, enhancing visibility and targeting capabilities.</p></p>
