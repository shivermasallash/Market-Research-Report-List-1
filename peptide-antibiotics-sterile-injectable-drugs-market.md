<p><p>The peptide antibiotics sterile injectable drugs market is divided into two main types: ribosomal and non-ribosomal synthesized peptides. Ribosomal synthesized peptides are produced through ribosomes, utilizing the genetic code to assemble amino acids into peptides, often resulting in well-characterized and biologically active compounds. In contrast, non-ribosomal synthesized peptides are generated by specific enzymes outside of ribosomes, allowing for diverse structures and unique properties. Both types are crucial in combating bacterial infections and are increasingly valued for their specificity and effectiveness.</p></p>
