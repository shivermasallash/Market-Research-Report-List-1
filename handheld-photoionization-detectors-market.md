<p><p>The handheld photoionization detectors market comprises two main types: single photoionization detectors and multi photoionization detectors. Single photoionization detectors focus on detecting specific volatile organic compounds (VOCs) using a single ionization source, offering portability and simplicity for field use. In contrast, multi photoionization detectors utilize multiple ionization sources to simultaneously identify and quantify various VOCs, providing enhanced sensitivity and specificity. Together, these devices serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and hazardous material detection.</p></p>
