<p><p>The plastic squeeze tubes market is primarily categorized into two main types: extruded plastic tubes and plastic laminate tubes. Extruded plastic tubes are made by forcing molten plastic through a die, resulting in a seamless and customizable product, ideal for various applications such as cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, plastic laminate tubes combine multiple layers of materials to enhance barrier properties, making them suitable for products requiring better protection against external elements. Both types offer versatility and convenience for consumers.</p></p>
