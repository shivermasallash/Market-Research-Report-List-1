<p><p>Endodontic pluggers and spreaders are essential dental instruments used in root canal therapy. Pluggers are utilized to compact gutta-percha and other filling materials within the canal, ensuring a complete seal. Spreaders, on the other hand, are designed to facilitate the lateral condensation of filling materials, promoting optimal sealing within the root canal system. </p><p>The Endodontic Pluggers and Spreaders Market is seeing significant growth driven by rising awareness of dental health, increasing prevalence of dental diseases, and advancements in endodontic technologies.
