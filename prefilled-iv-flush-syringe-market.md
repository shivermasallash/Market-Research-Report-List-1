<p><p>The prefilled IV flush syringe market primarily includes two types: saline IV flush syringes and heparin IV flush syringes. Saline IV flush syringes are used to maintain venous access or clear medications from IV lines, utilizing sterile saline solution. Heparin IV flush syringes contain heparin, an anticoagulant, to prevent blood clots in catheters. Both types are designed for convenience and increased safety, reducing the risk of contamination and ensuring accurate dosing in various medical settings.</p></p>
