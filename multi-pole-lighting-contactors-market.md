<p><p>The multi-pole lighting contactors market is divided into two main types: mechanically held and electrically held contactors. Mechanically held contactors use a physical mechanism to maintain their position, offering high reliability and reduced energy consumption. In contrast, electrically held contactors rely on a continuous electrical supply to stay engaged, providing more flexible control options but potentially higher energy costs. Both types are essential for efficiently managing lighting systems in commercial and industrial applications, ensuring optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
