<p><p>The de-aromatic solvent oil market is categorized into three types based on flash points: low, medium, and high. Low flash point solvents, typically below 21°C, are volatile and flammable, making them suitable for applications requiring quick evaporation. Medium flash point solvents, between 21°C to 60°C, offer a balance of volatility and stability, ideal for various industrial processes. High flash point solvents, above 60°C, provide enhanced safety and are used in applications where high-temperature resistance is essential.</p></p>
